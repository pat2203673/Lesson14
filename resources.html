Resources

Employment and Housing Solutions

Employment Resources

Job Training Programs

We partner with local organizations to provide comprehensive job training programs that prepare individuals for sustainable employment.

  • Construction and skilled trades training
  • Food service and hospitality certification
  • Administrative and office skills
  • Customer service training
  • Digital literacy and basic computer skills

Employment Placement Services

Our dedicated employment specialists work directly with local employers to connect our residents with job opportunities.

  • Direct employer partnerships
  • Job fair coordination
  • Interview preparation and practice
  • Resume and application assistance
  • Professional reference support

Financial Literacy

Essential financial skills training to help individuals manage their income and build financial stability.

  • Basic budgeting and money management
  • Bank account setup and maintenance
  • Credit repair and building strategies
  • Benefits enrollment assistance
  • Tax preparation help

Professional Development

Ongoing support to help individuals advance in their careers and achieve long-term employment success.

  • Leadership and communication skills
  • Professional networking opportunities
  • Continuing education resources
  • Career advancement counseling
  • Mentorship programs

Housing Solutions

Transitional Housing

Our transitional housing program provides stable, temporary housing while individuals work toward permanent solutions.

  • Individual and family units available
  • Up to 24-month stays
  • On-site case management
  • Life skills development
  • Gradual independence preparation

Permanent Housing Assistance

We help individuals and families find and secure permanent housing in the community.

  • Housing search assistance
  • Landlord relationship building
  • Security deposit and first month assistance
  • Rental application support
  • Housing voucher program navigation

Affordable Housing Resources

Connections to affordable housing options throughout the greater Phoenix area.

  • Low-income housing database
  • Public housing authority connections
  • Subsidized housing waitlist management
  • Housing choice voucher assistance
  • Senior and disability housing options

Housing Stability Support

Ongoing support to help individuals maintain their housing once they are permanently housed.

  • Tenant rights education
  • Utility assistance programs
  • Emergency rental assistance
  • Mediation services
  • Long-term follow-up support

Success Programs

Rapid Rehousing Program

An intensive program designed to quickly move people experiencing homelessness into permanent housing and achieve housing stability.

Program Features:

  • Short-term rental assistance
  • Housing search and placement services
  • Case management and stabilization
  • Flexible financial assistance
  • Connection to community resources

Permanent Supportive Housing

Long-term housing assistance combined with supportive services for individuals with disabilities or chronic conditions.

Program Features:

  • Ongoing rental assistance
  • Intensive case management
  • Healthcare coordination
  • Mental health and substance abuse support
  • Life skills development
