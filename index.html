Welcome to New Beginnings

Providing Hope and Shelter for Our Community

Our Mission

New Beginnings Shelter System is dedicated to helping homeless individuals and families in our community by providing safe shelter, essential services, and helping them on the road to permanent housing.

Our Services Get Help Now Learn about our shelter, food, healthcare, and employment services Get immediate assistance or contact us for emergency shelter

Essential Services We Provide

Safe Shelter

Clean, secure sleeping quarters with dignity and respect for all individuals.

Meals & Nutrition

Three nutritious meals daily plus snacks and special dietary accommodations.

Healthcare Access

Basic medical care, mental health support, and connections to healthcare providers.

Employment Support

Job training, resume assistance, and employment placement services.

Housing Assistance

Help finding permanent housing and assistance with applications and deposits.

Support Services

Case management, counseling, and connections to community resources.

About New Beginnings

Our Commitment

We believe that everyone deserves a safe place to sleep and the opportunity to rebuild their life. Our comprehensive approach addresses immediate needs while working toward long-term solutions.

Since our founding, we have helped thousands of individuals and families transition from homelessness to stable housing.

Community Impact

Working with local partners, government agencies, and volunteers, we create a network of support that extends far beyond our shelter walls.

Our programs are designed to address the root causes of homelessness and provide sustainable solutions for our community members.

Accessibility Statement
