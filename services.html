Detailed Service Information

Emergency Shelter Services

Available 24/7: Safe, clean sleeping accommodations with privacy and dignity. Separate facilities for men, women, and families with secure supervision.

Amenities: Hot showers, laundry facilities, hygiene supplies, and personal care items. Pet-friendly spaces available for service animals.

Nutrition and Meal Services

Daily Meals: Breakfast (7-9 AM), Lunch (12-2 PM), Dinner (6-8 PM) with snacks available throughout the day.

Special Diets: Vegetarian, vegan, religious dietary requirements, medical restrictions, and food allergy accommodations available.

Healthcare and Wellness

Medical Care: On-site nurse, health assessments, medication management, and healthcare provider referrals.

Mental Health: Individual counseling, group therapy, crisis intervention, and substance abuse support with trauma-informed care.

Employment and Training

Job Preparation: Resume writing, interview skills, job search assistance, and professional clothing donations.

Skills Training: Computer literacy, financial planning, life skills workshops, and educational support including GED assistance.

Accessibility Statement
