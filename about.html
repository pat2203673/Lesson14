About Us

Our Mission, Vision, and Impact

Our Story

Founded on Compassion and Hope

New Beginnings Shelter System was founded in 2010 by a group of community members who recognized the growing need for comprehensive homeless services in our area. What started as a small emergency shelter has grown into a comprehensive system of support that addresses the root causes of homelessness.

Growing to Meet Community Needs

Over the years, we have expanded our services based on the changing needs of our community. We have added job training programs, mental health services, and permanent housing assistance to create a complete pathway from homelessness to stable housing.

Community Partnership and Collaboration

Our success is built on strong partnerships with local businesses, healthcare providers, government agencies, and faith-based organizations. Together, we create a network of support that extends far beyond our shelter walls.

Mission and Vision

Our Mission

To provide safe, dignified shelter and comprehensive support services that help individuals and families experiencing homelessness achieve stable housing and self-sufficiency.

We are committed to treating every person with respect and dignity while addressing both immediate needs and long-term solutions.

Our Vision

A community where every person has access to safe, affordable housing and the support needed to maintain housing stability and achieve their personal goals.

We envision a future where homelessness is rare, brief, and non-recurring in our community.

Core Values

Dignity and Respect

Every person deserves to be treated with dignity and respect, regardless of their circumstances. We provide services with compassion and without judgment.

Comprehensive Care

We address the whole person, not just their housing needs, by providing comprehensive services that support physical, mental, and social well-being.

Community Partnership

We believe in the power of community collaboration and work closely with partners to create lasting solutions to homelessness.

Our Impact

People Served

Since our founding, New Beginnings Shelter System has provided services to thousands of individuals and families in our community.

  • Over 5,000 individuals served since 2010
  • Average of 150 people housed nightly
  • 1,200+ families assisted with housing
  • 2,500+ people connected to employment
  • 10,000+ meals served monthly

Success Outcomes

We measure our success by the long-term stability of the people we serve and their ability to maintain housing and employment.

  • 85% of clients maintain housing after one year
  • 70% of job placements lead to full-time employment
  • 90% of families remain stably housed
  • 95% client satisfaction rating
  • Low recidivism rates for shelter stays
